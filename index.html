ABOUT ME

My Purr-fect Introduction

I was born in June 10, 2022. I'm a full breed maine coon and have a colour of black and white fur. My owner name is Katrina and she's my favourite HOOMAN! I have a brother name "Scout" whom i both adore and hate at the same time.

I hate meeting new people and would hide every time I see them going near me, but don't worry I'm very nice...once I feel comfortable around a person I would let them pet me(not my belly tho).

My Favourite MEOW to do

Sleep

I love to sleep so meow! that's my most favourite meow to do even at day and night. I can sleep anywhere but where I usually go to is the washroom sink since i fit there nicely and it's very cool place to sleep into.

Food

I would always make sounds if I'm very hungry or when I see my owner putting food on my plate. I usually eat dry food anytime during the day and wet food at 8 pm every night with my brother, which I would eat faster than him and eat his food after :)

Touch

I love it when my owner touch my head and chin when they see me or usually I go to them. I would make a purr sounds and make my owner smile and hug me tight after which I hated the most... I usually try my best to escape.

Play

My favourite meow to play is fishing wand...I love it when my owner play it with me and would chase it till i get tired.
